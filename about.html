About

The Edo Soft Power Initiative (ESPI)

A Movement focused on how Edo and the Edo Diaspora can harness and optimize our unique, world-renowned and centuries-old creative and knowledge centered cultural heritage; and the adroit deployment of innovative, people and growth-focused mechanisms, to effectively harness the innate soft power dimensions of the former to engender inclusive growth and development for the Edo peoples.

Edo, The Creative Industry, Soft Power and The World

In recent years there has been a growing interest in the role that cultural and creative industries play in developing economies - both in terms of their economic contribution but also in connection with social change and cultural engagement.
